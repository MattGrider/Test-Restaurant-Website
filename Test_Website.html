About Us

Cubano is located in the heart of the Chicago Loop near the “L” tracks, financial and bustling theater district.

We offer classic and a modern take on Cuban sandwiches as well as new style Latin sandwiches, soups, salads and fries. Cubano Latín Street Food is a quick service restaurant where all the food is hand-made fresh everyday, using local high quality meats, and ingredients. Please come on by and check out how we pride ourselves in bringing the Chicago Loop one of quickest, friendliest, authentic places to eat.
